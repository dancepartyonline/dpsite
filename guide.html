Guide

Your first step to connect to DanceParty

Legacy

This guide will help you on how to connect to Legacy.

Requirements
  • A real Wii or Dolphin Emulator
  • Legal owned copy of Just Dance game between 2016-2018
  • Python
We use Wiimmfi for our authentication server so if you are a Dolphin user, you need to import your Wii's dumped NAND to Dolphin in order to connect. To import your NAND to Dolphin, follow this guide.
  1. Extract your game's content by using either Dolphin or any other tool you prefer, you need to access DATA/sys folder for your main.dol file.
  2. Download the Patcher
  3. Use the patcher to select your main.dol file and let it do the magic for you.
  4. Once you're done, you can now burn your game back to it's original format and enjoy DanceParty!
For any help or any question, you can join our Discord server.
